About AFICS/NY

The Association of Former International Civil Servants (New York) (AFICS/NY) is a retiree organization that supports United Nations programs and principles.

It promotes these goals while assisting former international civil servants and those about to separate from service. It also represents the interests of its members within the system, especially regarding pension and health insurance (ASHI) matters.

Our Mission

AFICS/NY aims to:

  • Support United Nations programs and principles
  • Assist former international civil servants and those about to separate from service
  • Represent the interests of its members within the UN system
  • Foster social and personal relationships among members
  • Promote the well-being of its members
  • Encourage mutual support among the membership
